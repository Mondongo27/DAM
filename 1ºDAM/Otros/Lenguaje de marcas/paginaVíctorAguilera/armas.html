Below you will find some of the most iconic weapons from Dark Souls. Each one can be upgraded using Titanite and has unique scaling attributes.

Claymore

The Claymore is a versatile greatsword found on the bridge of the Undead Burg. It deals 138 base damage and scales well with STR/DEX.

Can be upgraded to +15 with titanite. Do not use with low endurance!
